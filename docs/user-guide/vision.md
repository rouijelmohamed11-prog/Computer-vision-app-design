# Vision Tools

The Vision Pipeline is one of the most advanced features of VisionStudioAI, leveraging Computer Vision to automate complex image editing tasks.

## Real-time Filters

Apply non-destructive filters to any image element from the **Properties** panel:

- **Grayscale**: Classic black and white conversion.
- **Blur**: Soften images for backgrounds.
- **Sharpen**: Enhance details and clarity.
- **Edges**: Extract structural outlines.

## Advanced AI Tools

These tools use sophisticated algorithms to understand image content:

### 1. Background Removal
Automatically isolate the main subject of an image. Perfect for creating product shots or composite designs.

### 2. Face Detection
Identifies human faces within an image. Useful for automated cropping or applying specific effects to portraits.

### 3. Smart Cropping
Analyzes the composition of an image to perform a "center-of-interest" crop, ensuring the most important parts are preserved.

### 4. Palette Extraction
Analyzes an image and generates a 5-color palette based on the dominant and secondary colors present in the photo. These colors can then be applied to other elements in your design for perfect harmony.

## Image Adjustments

Fine-tune your visuals with standard adjustments:
- **Brightness**
- **Contrast**
- **Saturation**
