# AI-Driven Design

VisionStudioAI isn't just an editor; it's a creative partner. The AI Design system allows you to generate layouts and styles using natural language.

## The Prompt Bar

Located at the top of the interface, the Prompt Bar is where you communicate your vision.

### How to use it
1.  Type a description of what you want (e.g., "Create a cyberpunk header for a tech blog").
2.  Press `Enter` or click **✦ Generate**.
3.  The NLP engine parses your intent and the AI Reasoning system applies the appropriate design rules.

## Design Reasoning

The AI understands specific aesthetic "moods" and applies rules based on them:

| Mood | Characteristics | Actions Taken |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Luxury** | Elegant, Spacious, Dark | Serif fonts, dark palettes, increased white space. |
| **Cyberpunk** | Bold, High-Contrast, Neon | Futuristic fonts, glow effects, neon palettes. |
| **Minimal** | Clean, Functional, Light | Sans-serif light fonts, neutral palettes, clutter removal. |

## AI Suggestions

The **Intelligence** tab in the left sidebar provides context-aware suggestions based on your current design. It can suggest:
- Complementary color schemes.
- Layout improvements.
- Font pairings.
