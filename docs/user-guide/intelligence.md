# Intelligence & Style Transformation

VisionStudioAI uses "Design Intelligence" to automate the styling process.

## Context-Aware Design

The AI doesn't just look at individual commands; it looks at the **History** of your session.

### Example Workflow
1.  **Prompt**: "Create a banner for a summer sale."
    *   *AI result*: Adds text and a basic layout.
2.  **Prompt**: "Make it minimal."
    *   *AI result*: The AI remembers the "summer sale" context but transforms the font to a light sans-serif and clears unnecessary clutter.

## Style Definitions

Here is a detailed breakdown of what the AI does for each style:

### ✦ Luxury
*   **Typography**: Switches to elegant Serif fonts (like Playfair Display or Times New Roman).
*   **Composition**: Centers elements and adds significant padding (white space).
*   **Color**: Prefers dark backgrounds with high-contrast text.

### ✦ Cyberpunk
*   **Typography**: Switches to bold, wide, or futuristic fonts.
*   **Effects**: Automatically applies "Glow" or neon-like attributes to shapes.
*   **Color**: Switches to a palette of deep purples, neon pinks, and cyans.

### ✦ Minimal
*   **Typography**: Uses clean, thin Sans-Serif fonts.
*   **Composition**: Strict alignment and removal of decorative elements.
*   **Color**: Neutral tones, grays, and whites.

## AI Suggestions Panel

The **Intelligence** tab (found in the left sidebar) provides real-time suggestions:
*   **Palette Match**: Suggests colors that look good with your existing elements.
*   **Font Pairing**: Suggests a secondary font that complements your primary selection.
*   **Layout Fix**: Offers to auto-align or balance the composition of the selected elements.
